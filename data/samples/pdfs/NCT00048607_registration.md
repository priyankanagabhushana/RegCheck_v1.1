# Registration: A Double-Blind, Multicenter, Placebo and Active-Controlled, Acute Extension Study of 2 Doses of MK-0869 in the Treatment of Patients With Major Depressive Disorder

## ClinicalTrials.gov ID: NCT00048607
## Status: COMPLETED
## Sample Size: N=600

## Primary Outcomes
- HAMD-17 total score at week 8. Tolerability.

## Secondary Outcomes
- CGI-I Scale score at week 8 and Sheehan Disability Scale at week 8

## Eligibility Criteria
Patients with Major Depressive Disorder
